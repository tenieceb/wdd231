Crochet Me Knot Buzey – Project Site Plan

Site Name

Crochet Me Knot - Teniece Busenbark

Site Purpose

This website will promote my crochet side-business by showcasing handmade crochet products, providing a rotating “free pattern of the day” from external sources, and allowing customers to place orders for pre-made or custom items.

Target Audience

This site is for people who love handmade goods, are looking for unique gifts, enjoy crochet patterns, or are interested in placing custom orders. This includes crafters, gift buyers, and anyone who appreciates handmade artistry.

Site Pages

  • Home: Overview of my crochet story and a random “free pattern of the day” pulled from a curated set or API.
  • Products: JSON-powered product list with filters (e.g., hats, scarves, blankets) and a search bar.
  • Order: A form where users can select a product, customize an order, and provide contact information.

Color Scheme & Fonts

  • Colors: Sage green (#A3B18A), blush pink (#F2C6C2), cream (#FFF5E1), soft gray for accents
  • Fonts: Roboto for body text, Dancing Script or Quicksand for headings
